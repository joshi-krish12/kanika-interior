Founded in 2015, Kanika Interior Solutions LLP has established itself as a premier interior design and furniture manufacturing company in Kolkata, West Bengal. Our journey began with a vision to transform spaces with innovative design solutions that blend functionality with aesthetics.

Over the years, we have successfully completed numerous projects for both residential and commercial clients, earning a reputation for excellence in design, quality craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

At Kanika Interior Solutions, our mission is to create inspired spaces that reflect our clients' personalities, enhance their lifestyles, and add value to their properties. We are committed to delivering exceptional design solutions with an unwavering focus on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of design to create unique and contemporary spaces that stand the test of time.

Quality

We are committed to using the finest materials and craftsmanship in all our projects to ensure durability and excellence.

Collaboration

We believe in working closely with our clients to understand their needs and bring their vision to life.

Our Design Process

01

Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your requirements, preferences, and budget constraints. This helps us create a design brief that aligns with your vision.

02

Concept Development

Our design team creates initial concepts based on your brief, incorporating space planning, color schemes, material selections, and furniture layouts.

03

Design Presentation

We present detailed design proposals including 3D visualizations, material samples, and cost estimates for your approval and feedback.

04

Implementation

Once the design is approved, our execution team takes over to bring the design to life, ensuring quality workmanship and attention to detail at every step.

05

Final Handover

After a thorough quality check, we hand over the completed project to you, providing maintenance advice and ongoing support as needed.
