Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free consultations?

+

Yes, we offer a complimentary initial consultation to discuss your project requirements and provide a general overview of our services. This can be conducted at our office, on-site, or virtually based on your preference.

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve Kolkata and surrounding areas in West Bengal. However, we also undertake projects in other major cities across India depending on the scope and scale of the project. For projects outside Kolkata, additional travel and accommodation charges may apply.

How quickly can you start a new project?

+

Our project timeline depends on our current workload and the complexity of your project. Typically, we can begin the design phase within 1-2 weeks of confirming the project details and receiving the initial payment. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

What information should I prepare for our first meeting?

+

To make our first meeting productive, it helps to have the following information ready: your budget range, timeline expectations, any inspiration images or ideas you have, floor plans if available, and a clear understanding of your requirements and preferences. The more information you can provide, the better we can address your needs.
