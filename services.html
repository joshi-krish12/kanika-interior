Our Service Process

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with an in-depth discussion to understand your requirements, preferences, and budget. This helps us establish clear objectives for your project.

2

Site Assessment

Our team visits your space to assess the site conditions, take measurements, and identify any potential challenges or opportunities.

3

Proposal & Agreement

Based on our assessment, we prepare a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work, timeline, and cost estimates for your approval.

4

Design Development

Our design team creates concept designs, material selections, and detailed specifications for your project based on your approved proposal.

5

Implementation

Upon your approval, we proceed with the execution of the project, coordinating all aspects of construction, installation, and finishing.

6

Quality Inspection

We conduct thorough quality checks to ensure that every detail meets our high standards before the final handover.

7

Project Completion

We hand over the completed project to you, providing necessary documentation, maintenance advice, and follow-up support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical interior design project take?

+

The duration varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. A simple room redesign might take 4-6 weeks, while a complete home or commercial space renovation could take 3-6 months or more. During the initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific project requirements.

Do you work with clients who have a limited budget?

+

Yes, we work with clients across various budget ranges. We believe good design should be accessible and can help you prioritize elements to achieve maximum impact within your budget constraints. We'll be transparent about costs and help you make informed decisions to get the best value for your investment.

Can you work with my existing furniture and decor?

+

Absolutely! We're happy to incorporate your existing pieces into the new design. We'll assess your current furniture and decor items and suggest ways to integrate them with new elements for a cohesive look. This approach is both sustainable and budget-friendly.

Do you offer virtual design services?

+

Yes, we offer virtual design services for clients who prefer remote collaboration. Through video consultations, digital mood boards, 3D renderings, and detailed specifications, we can deliver comprehensive design solutions without in-person meetings. This is particularly useful for clients in different locations or those with busy schedules.

What is the warranty period for your custom furniture?

+

Our custom furniture comes with a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. This covers issues related to construction, materials, and workmanship under normal use conditions. We also offer maintenance services to ensure the longevity of your furniture beyond the warranty period.

How do you handle project management and coordination?

+

We provide comprehensive project management services to ensure smooth execution. This includes coordinating with contractors, vendors, and other professionals involved in your project. We maintain regular communication, provide progress updates, and address any issues promptly to keep your project on track and within budget.
